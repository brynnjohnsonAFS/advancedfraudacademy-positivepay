Honest questions

A few common questions before you reach out.

Will you try to sell me on Modern Positive Pay during the call?

No. If Modern Positive Pay is the right move for your FI given your situation, we will say so. If it is not, we will say so. The session is meant to help you think clearly about the decision, not to push you toward one.

Do I need to bring anyone from Finance, IT, or Operations?

No. Most of these sessions are one-on-one with the treasury manager or VP of treasury. We will help you build what you need to bring back to Finance, IT, or whoever else has a say in the decision. If it makes sense to bring someone else to a later conversation, we will figure that out together.

What happens after the call?

Whatever makes sense for you. Some people walk away with the math and the peer context and that is all they needed. Some want a follow-up email with what we discussed. Some are ready to look at Modern Positive Pay seriously for their FI and we go from there. You decide what is useful.

How prepared do I need to be?

If you've already run the calculator, you're prepared. If not, run it before the call so we have your numbers to anchor on — takes 60 seconds. Beyond that, just bring your situation and your questions.

What if I'm just curious and not actively evaluating?

Welcome. A lot of these sessions are with FIs that are still figuring out whether they want to look at this seriously. The peer context is useful at that stage. We will follow up occasionally with new information that might matter to you, and you reach out when you are ready.
