Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The Modern Positive Pay Academy is operated by Advanced Fraud Solutions, Inc. ("AFS," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this site.

Information we collect

When you enroll in the Academy or claim a certificate, we collect: your first and last name, work email address, institution name, job role, and asset size. We also collect standard analytics data (pages visited, time on site) via Pardot.

How we use it

We use your information to send Academy-related communications (lesson updates, new track announcements, peer benchmarks). We do not sell your personal information to third parties. AFS may contact you about its products and services. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your Academy enrollment is active, or until you request deletion. To request deletion, email privacy@advancedfraudsolutions.com.

Cookies and tracking

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising, including Pardot, Google Analytics, and the LinkedIn Insight Tag. These help us understand how visitors use the Academy and measure the performance of our marketing. By using this site, you consent to this tracking. You may disable cookies in your browser settings. For full details on AFS's cookie and advertising practices, see the AFS Privacy Policy.
